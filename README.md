## What Is This?

An **AI skill file** is a structured knowledge document that you upload to an AI chat assistant (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, etc.) to give it deep domain expertise. Think of it as a portable "brain upgrade" for your AI.

`SKILL.md` is a single Markdown file (~47KB, 800+ lines) containing:

- **Role-calibrated responses** that adapt to your seniority level (APM through CPO)
- **30+ product management frameworks** with decision logic for when to use each
- **Confidence calibration** — every output includes a confidence level (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW) with reasoning
- **Bias disclosures** — explicit acknowledgment of 5 reasoning bias dimensions
- **Industry-specific calibration** for SaaS, fintech, healthcare, marketplace, hardware, and more
- **Communication templates** (PRDs, strategy docs, OKRs, business plans)
- **Anti-patterns and failure modes** to avoid common PM mistakes
- **Career development framework** with a skills matrix from APM to CPO

## How to Use

### ChatGPT (OpenAI)
1. Open a new conversation
2. Click the attachment/paperclip icon
3. Upload `SKILL.md`
4. Start asking product management questions — the model will use the skill automatically

### Claude (Anthropic)
1. Open a new conversation
2. Click the attachment icon
3. Upload `SKILL.md`
4. Begin your PM conversation

### Perplexity
1. Navigate to your Perplexity library or skill settings
2. Upload `SKILL.md` as a custom skill
3. The skill activates automatically when PM-related questions are detected

### Gemini (Google)
1. Open a new conversation
2. Upload `SKILL.md` as an attachment
3. Ask your product management questions

### Any Other AI Assistant
If the platform supports file uploads or custom instructions, upload `SKILL.md` or paste its contents into the system/custom instructions field.

### Tips for Best Results
- **State your seniority level** — e.g., "I'm a Senior PM at a fintech startup" — so the skill calibrates its response depth and complexity
- **Mention your industry** — the skill adjusts frameworks and examples for SaaS, healthcare, fintech, marketplace, hardware, and more
- **Ask for specific deliverables** — "Write me a PRD for X" or "Help me set OKRs for Q3" to get structured template outputs
- **Challenge the output** — the skill includes contrarian views and anti-patterns by design; ask "What could go wrong?" to surface them

## Testing and Validation

The skill file was rigorously tested across **6 diverse scenarios** spanning all seniority levels and industries:

| Scenario | Seniority | Industry | Score |
|----------|-----------|----------|-------|
| User Stories for Notification Feature | APM | B2B SaaS | 8.0/10 |
| Pricing Strategy for New Market | PM | Fintech | 9.0/10 |
| Outcome-Based Roadmap | Senior PM | Healthcare | 9.0/10 |
| Feature Factory Transformation | Director | Enterprise | 9.3/10 |
| Board Presentation on Product Strategy | CPO | Multi-product | 9.0/10 |
| AI Feature Discovery and Validation | AI PM | AI/ML Platform | 9.3/10 |

**Overall Score: 8.9 / 10**

### Evaluation Criteria
Each scenario was scored on 7 dimensions:
1. **Seniority Detection** — correct calibration to the user's level
2. **Framework Selection** — appropriate 2-3 frameworks chosen (not too many, not too few)
3. **Confidence and Bias Calibration** — confidence tags and bias disclosures present and accurate
4. **Actionability** — concrete next steps the user can take immediately
5. **Industry Adaptation** — industry-specific knowledge reflected
6. **Anti-Pattern Avoidance** — common failure modes warned against
7. **Contrarian View** — at least one alternative perspective offered
